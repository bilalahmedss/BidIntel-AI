Nexus Advisory - Company Profile

Nexus Advisory is an internationally based consulting firm with offices in Pakistan and the UK.

The firm supports public-sector and development programs in digital financial services, payment systems reform, financial inclusion strategy, and policy implementation.

Team Nationality and Eligibility Declaration
- All team members are Pakistani or British nationals.
- No team member is a national, permanent resident, or originating from India or Israel.

Tender Eligibility Statement
- Nexus Advisory is eligible to bid on international and local tenders.
- Nexus Advisory maintains active legal and tax registrations required for domestic and international assignments.
