Consortium Partnership Letter

Date: 12 March 2026

To Whom It May Concern,

This letter confirms the partnership arrangement between Nexus Advisory (Private) Limited and Karachi Research Associates (KRA) for public-sector consulting assignments in Pakistan, including digital financial services and payment systems engagements.

1) Parties
- Lead Firm: Nexus Advisory (Private) Limited
- Executing Partner: Karachi Research Associates (KRA)

2) Consortium Roles and Responsibilities
- Nexus Advisory shall serve as the lead firm and principal point of contact for the Client.
- KRA shall act as the local executing partner and provide in-country implementation support, stakeholder coordination, field logistics, and local research execution.
- Both firms agree to collaborate on proposal preparation, project planning, resource mobilization, quality assurance, and delivery.

3) Liability and Risk Allocation
- The consortium parties agree to joint and several liability for all contractual obligations arising from assignments undertaken jointly.
- Each party shall ensure compliance with applicable laws, professional standards, and contractual commitments.

4) Governance and Coordination
- A joint project steering arrangement shall be established for assignment oversight.
- Authorized representatives from both firms shall approve key deliverables and contractual decisions requiring consortium concurrence.

5) Validity
- This partnership letter is valid for the current bid and any resulting contract period unless superseded by a more detailed consortium agreement.
